this interactive story focuses on an aspiring nation-state slowly absorbing surrounding city-states. this website contains an interactive topographical map of the developing nation-state and its new residents who are struggling to synthesize. a focus has been placed on mistranslation, having to adopt a new structure of common sense, and the dissolution of small emotional communities i.e. the end of a city-state.
